Rules

General Moderation Guides

  • Never punish without reason or valid evidence.
  • Always gather information from both parties.
  • Use in-game commands like view to observe situations.
  • Refer to server rules when making decisions.

Staff Communication & Behavior

  • Keep staff chat professional.
  • Do not argue with other staff in public channels.
  • Use DMs or tickets for internal disagreements.
  • Respect all roles, regardless of rank.
