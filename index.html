Home

Welcome to the staff team of WSRP. This handbook outlines everything you need to know as a staff member to maintain professionalism, enforce
rules, and support the community effectively.

The code to join ingame is: WSRPC.


Staff Expectations

  • Act mature and professional at all times.
  • Enforce rules fairly without bias or favoritism.
  • Be active and responsive during your shifts or assigned duties.

Represent the server in a respectful and positive manner.


Chain of Command

  1. Moderation Team
  2. Administration Team
  3. Internal Affairs Team
  4. Supervisor Team
  5. Management Team
  6. Leadership Team
  7. Chief of the board
  8. Board of Directors
  9. Department Manager
  10. Ownership Team
Always follow the chain of command when escalating issues.

Promotions and Infractions

Promotions are based on activity, professionalism, and judgment.

Final notes

Thank you for volunteering your time to help our community. Your role is vital in keeping the server fun, fair, and safe for everyone.

If you're ever unsure about a situation, ask a higher-ranking staff member before acting.

Stay professional. Stay fair. Keep the community strong.




Rules

General Moderation Guides

  • Never punish without reason or valid evidence.
  • Always gather information from both parties.
  • Use in-game commands like view to observe situations.
  • Refer to server rules when making decisions.

Staff Communication & Behavior

  • Keep staff chat professional.
  • Do not argue with other staff in public channels.
  • Use DMs or tickets for internal disagreements.
  • Respect all roles, regardless of rank.

Infractions

This Infraction Handbook ensures fair and just infractions for every staff member within the server. If the handbook does not specifically state a harsher infraction for a 2nd, 3rd, 4th, etc., offense, the infraction will remain the same.

Warnings

  • Failure to Respect CoC (Chain of Command)
  • Shift Grinding (15+ Minutes)
  • Chatting in Logging Channels
  • Being On-Duty While AFK
  • Logging wrong punishment
  • Randomly Speaking in Ticket
  • Using heal even with off duty commands
  • Using off duty commands on, on-duty staff (without just cause)
  • Asking for promotions

Strikes

  • Receiving a Warning Off-Duty (In-Game)
  • Staff Disrespect (1st Offense)
  • Staff Disrespect (2nd Offense)
  • Violating the Staff Code of Conduct (Mild)
  • Violating the Staff Handbook (Mild)
  • Member Disrespect (1st Offense)
  • Member Disrespect (2nd Offense)
  • Shift Grinding (1 Hour)
  • Shift Grinding (12 Hour = 2 strikes)
  • Using Commands While Off-Duty
  • Failure to Log In-Game Punishments
  • Unprofessional
  • Abuse of Commands (Minor)
  • Inactivity / Failure to Meet Quota (Minor)
  • Logging Wrong Punishment 2nd Offense or more
  • Randomly Speaking in Ticket 2nd offense or more
  • Spamming Mod calls but not handling them
  • Trolling Staff Applications (2 strikes)
  • Leadership Disrespect (Minor)

Demotions

  • Staff Disrespect (3rd Offense)
  • Member Disrespect (3rd Offense)
  • Violating the Staff Code of Conduct (Major)
  • Violating the Staff Handbook (Major)
  • Abuse of Commands (Major)
  • Inactivity / Failure to Meet Quota (Major)
  • Leadership Disrespect (Major)

Terminations

  • Violating the Staff Code of Conduct (Major)
  • Violating the Staff Handbook (Extreme)
  • Adding Roles to Oneself (Major)
  • Issuing Unlogged Infractions / Promotions (Major)
  • Abuse of Commands (Extreme)
  • Inactivity / Failure to Meet Quota (Extreme)

Promotions

This Promotion Handbook ensures fair and just promotions for every staff member within the server. If the handbook does not specifically state faster promotion eligibility for repeated excellence, the requirements will remain the same.

Minor promotions (Recognition & Trust Growth)

These are small advancements typically within the same tier or to the next entry rank.

  • Consistent Professionalism in Tickets
  • Meeting Weekly Quota (3 Weeks Consecutive)
  • Positive Community Feedback (Multiple Members)
  • Completing Assigned Trainings
  • Going Above Expectations in Duties
  • Assisting Lower-Ranked Staff With Knowledge

Major Promotions (Leadership Growth)

These are significant advancements to higher staff levels (Supervisor, Management, or Directive)

  • Serving 1 Month Without Infractions
  • Hosting Trainings & Sessions Successfully (3+)
  • Outstanding Professionalism & Maturity
  • Consistently Exceeding Quotas (4 Weeks Consecutive)
  • Positive Feedback From Leadership Team
  • Taking Initiative in Events, Projects, or Department Growth

Leadership Promotions (HR, Directive, & Executive)

Reserved for exceptional staff who demonstrate long-term dedication and leadership ability.

  • 2+ Months of Active Service Without Infractions
  • Proven Record of Hosting Events/Trainings Regularly
  • Demonstrated Conflict Resolution Skills
  • Mentorship of Lower Staff Ranks
  • Leadership Approval & Vote of Confidence

Automatic Promotions

These are guaranteed promotions when the listed conditions are met.

  • Staff Member of the Month
  • Completing Leadership Training Program
  • Outstanding Event Contribution (Approved by Directive/Exec)
  • Successful Department Leadership (if assigned)

Promotion Denials & Delays

Promotions may be denied or delayed if any of the following apply:

  • Active Infractions Within the Last 2 Weeks
  • Failure to Meet Basic Quotas
  • Negative Community Feedback
  • Poor Professionalism in Tickets or Sessions

Role Removal

Roles may be removed if promotions were gained unfairly or performance drops significantly.

  • Failure to Maintain Activity Post-Promotion
  • Abuse of Elevated Permissions
  • Multiple Reports of Leadership Abuse
  • Failure to Meet Standards of Conduct
